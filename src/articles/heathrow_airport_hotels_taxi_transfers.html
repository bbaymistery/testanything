Heathrow Airport Hotels

Heathrow is the UK’s largest airport, with over 60 million passengers passing through its five terminals each year. An airport of this size needs hotel facilities to match and Heathrow certainly does not disappoint in this regard. With in the region of 35 hotels and guest houses both on the site of the airport and in the immediate vicinity, there’s something to suit all tastes and pockets.

Amongst the best value are the Comfort Inn, the Novotel and the Ibis, with rooms typically available from under £50 per night. The latter is situated just 1 mile from Terminals 1, 2 and 3, and boasts an a la carte restaurant, bar with Sky Sports and a shuttle bus every 15 minutes, to/from the Airport.

If it’s luxury you’re after, the Crowne Plaza is a good option. With an impressive list of leisure facilities including a 16m indoor pool, gym, sauna and 9-hole golf course, your holiday starts here! For ease of access to the Airport, the Renaissance Heathrow is one of the nearest, just 0.5 miles away. It’s so close, it’s actually possible see the runway from the hotel -perfect for plane spotters! There is a genuinely plenty of choice when it comes to Heathrow Airport hotels. Taxi is the obvious choice if you want to ensure a prompt and stress free journey to the Airport/your hotel.
