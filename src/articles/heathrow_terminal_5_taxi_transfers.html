Facilities at Heathrow Terminal 5

The newest of Heathrow’s terminals, the impressive Terminal 5 cost over £4bn to construct and was opened in March 2008.

Occupying a prime position on the western side of the site with an area of 353,020m sq, the terminal consists of a 4-storey main building with two satellite buildings linked via an underground transit system. To ensure smooth passage for your Heathrow airport taxi, a dedicated motorway spur for access to Terminal 5 has been built between junctions 14 and 15 of the M25.
