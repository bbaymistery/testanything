Gatwick to Heathrow Transfer & Travel

Gatwick Airport consists of 2 terminals. We are providing taxi services from both Gatwick Terminals, for all arriving passengers our pick-up point is always inside the terminals.

Which Terminal?

If you are unaware of which terminal your flight is scheduled to arrive into or depart, you can visit the LGW official website. Where live flight statuses will be shown 24 hours a day, in order for you to allocate which terminal your flight will be approaching into, either north or south terminal. Alternatively you can visit our which terminal page.

Where is the arrival lounge at Gatwick?

  • ■Terminal North Terminal: Arrivals is located on the ground floor.   
  • ■ South Terminal: International arrival is located to the left of the terminal.

Meeting Points with Driver

When arriving at Gatwick terminal, soon after going through Customs and Passport control and baggage claim area, you will arrive at the arrivals hall where your driver will be waiting with our Companies name board with your name. If for some reason you cannot establish your Driver, you don’t need to panic, simply make your way to the Airport Information Desk. When arriving at the information desk please ask one of the staff to contact us on 0208 688 77 44 so then we can contact the Driver and allocate where he/she is instantly. Our lines are open 24 hours a day 365 days a year. If there are any problems or confusion prior to your pickup please so not hesitate to contact us.

 

    Locating Information Desk at Gatwick:

  • ■ North Terminal: You will find the Information Desk allocated on the left hand side as you finally arrive at the arrivals hall.
  • ■ South Terminal: You will find the Information Desk allocated in the main arrivals area as you finally arrive at the arrivals hall.

Journey Time between Gatwick and Heathrow:

Gatwick to Heathrow is approximately 45 miles so you should allow up to an hour to arrive to your Destination, Our Drivers use the M23 and M25 for transfers between the airports, Please take into account the Rush hour time which is from about 07:00 till 09:30, so Please allow yourself more time to get to the Airport so there will be enough time to catch your flight.

Gatwick/Heathrow Airport to Hotel:

We also Provide Service to be picked up from the Hotel going to the Airport and also service from the Airport to a Hotel.
