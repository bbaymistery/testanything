Facilities at Southampton Port

Southampton enjoys a convenient location, 75 miles (120 km) south of London and approximately 21 miles (33km) from the Channel Ports of Dover and Folkstone.

Within easy reach of Southampton city centre, the Cruise Port comprises Eastern and Western Docks, each containing two cruise terminals.

Facilities are spread across the four terminals, each offering refreshment points (buffet restaurants and licensed café/bars), rest room and viewing galleries/vantage points from which to watch departing ships. There is even a shuttle service to the city centre if you find you have some extra time for shopping following your Southampton taxi transfer.
