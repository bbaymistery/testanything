Facilities at Dover Port

A port since Roman times, Dover is a transport hub of huge significance and the main gateway from the south of England to the continent. The Port consists of ferry and cruise terminals and accommodates freight and passenger ships of varying sizes. Facilities on site include a Travelex currency exchange office, branch of NatWest Bank, with ATM machines, plus an assortment of eating and refreshment outlets. Look out for Burger King, Café Ritazza and Barnacles Bar amongst others.
Cruise and Ferry Services from Dover Port

Round the clock cross-channel ferry services operate from the Port of Dover, with some 55 crossings per 24 hours. Ferries are operated by P&O, Seafrance, LD Lines and Norfolk Lines, with arrival points in France include Boulogne, Calais and Dunkerque.

We also provide transfers to Dover cruise terminal, situated in the western docks of the port. Cruise liners that operate from Dover include Carnival, Regent, Saga, Orient, Norwegian Cruise Line, Swan Hellenic, Crystal, Fred Olsen, Costa and Oceana.
