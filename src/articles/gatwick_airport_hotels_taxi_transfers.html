Gatwick Airport Hotels

Over 35 million passengers pass through Gatwick annually and with flights arriving and departing round the clock, many choose to begin or round-off their holiday with a stay at one of over 25 Gatwick Airport Hotels. Taxi is far and away the most convenient means of reaching your hotel, and Heathrow-gatwick-transfers.com provides a competitively priced, executive transfer service.

There are several hotels to choose from, some on the site of the Airport itself, others a short shuttle ride away. Those on a budget might like to consider the two-star Ibis, a great value stopover. The Gatwick branch of Travelodge is set 2 miles from the Airport and is ideal for those seeking comfort at a great price.

Mid-range options include the 3-star Gatwick Express Hotel by Holiday Inn. Rooms are well equipped, with telephone satellite television, hairdryer and tea/coffee making facilities as standard. At the luxury end of the market, there is the Sofitel, formerly Le Meridian, recently refurbished and offering a choice of three restaurants for fine dining. The 4-star Gatwick Hilton is situated a four-minute stroll by covered walkway from the South Terminal building.

The majority of hotels lay on regular free or low-cost shuttle services to and from Gatwick Airport.
