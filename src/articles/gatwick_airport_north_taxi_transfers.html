Facilities at Gatwick North Terminal-Gatwick Insight


Gatwick Airport is London’s 2nd biggest and Europe’s 8th in terms of passenger traffic. The Airport is home to the worlds busiest single-use runway and consists of two terminals, South and North, connected via a 1km-long shuttle train. The North Terminal is the newer of the two.

Upon transfer to Gatwick Airport, you will be presented with an impressive choice of facilities, both before and after the security gates. All your holiday needs are catered for, from cash machines and currency exchange, right through to concierge services.
