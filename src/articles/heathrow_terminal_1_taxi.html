Facilities at Heathrow Terminal 1

Opened by Queen Elizabeth II in 1969, Terminal 1 underwent substantial redevelopment in 2005, the Eastern extension almost doubling lounge and retail space. Upon arrival at Terminal 1 following your smooth and comfortable Heathrow transfer, you’ll find an array of facilities on site.

From essentials such as cash machines, payphone and Internet access points, to concierge services, currency exchange and hotel reservations desks. Terminal 1 is also home to several eateries including Costa, Caffe Nero, Café Rouge, Est, Caviar House and Giraffe.

Don’t forget to organise your Heathrow Airport taxi transfer to allow for plenty of time to browse Terminal 1’s impressive 40+ shops: look out for classic British designer brands Mulberry and Burberry as well as high street favourites French Connection and Ted Baker. Other stores include Boots, Dixons, HMV, Hamleys WH Smith and Sunglasses Hut.
