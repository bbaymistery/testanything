Facilities at Heathrow Terminal 4

Heathrow Airport is the largest in the UK and forth busiest in the world in terms of passenger numbers. The length of your transfer to Heathrow Airport will be approximately 22 km, 1 hour in duration. West of central London and occupying a site some 12.4 square kilometres in area, the Airport comprises 5 terminals, numbered from 1 to 5.

Terminal 4 is situated to the south of the plot. Originally opened in 1986, a recent £200m facelift means it now accommodates some 45 airlines, with extended check-in areas and departure lounges for your added convenience and comfort.

The Terminal is home to an impressive mix of high street and designer concessions, with everything from Tie Rack, Dixons and WH Smith to LK Bennett, Hackett and Jimmy Choo to complete the first class retail experience. We advise that you allow plenty of time when booking your Heathrow Airport taxi transfer, to ensure you have the chance to visit them all. Food outlets include Garfunkel’s, Café Rouge, Starbucks and Wetherspoons. The usual currency exchange, Internet, ATM and concierge facilities can also be found here.
