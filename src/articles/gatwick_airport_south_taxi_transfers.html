Facilities at Gatwick South Terminal-Gatwick Insight

London’s 2nd busiest, the 8th in Europe, Gatwick Airport comprises two terminal buildings, the North and South. The Airport is situated near Crawley, West Sussex, just to the south of London. Gatwick comes from the Anglo-Saxon words for “goat” and “farm” and is thought to derive from the name of the manor originally located on the site.

Gatwick’s South Terminal offers all the usual facilities and amenities you would expect from an airport of this size and importance. There are several cash machines, Internet access points and currency exchange bureaus for your travelling convenience.

In addition, you will find a choice of food and refreshment outlets catering for all tastes and pockets including Apostrophe, Caffe Nero, Pret A Manger and Café Rouge
