Gatwick to Heathrow Shuttle

HGT provides Airport shuttle service between Heathrow and Gatwick airport, Shuttle prices are starting from £55.00 per person. We are providing VW Transporter or Mercedes Vito for this service, Our driver will be collecting you inside the terminal, our meeting points are as follows,

Meeting Point Gatwick South

Meeting point at Gatwick South Terminal is “Airport Information Desk”, located just in front of the arrival barriers. Our driver will assist you to the vehicle.

Meeting Point Gatwick North

Meeting point at Gatwick North Terminal is “Airport Information Desk”, located just in front of the arrival barriers. Our driver will assist you to the vehicle

Meeting Point Heathrow Terminal 1

Heathrow Terminal 1 meeting point is Information desk, Located on the right hand side of arrivals hall, (international arrivals-ground floor.

Meeting Point Heathrow Terminal 3

Meeting point at Heathrow Airport Terminal 3 In the seated area in the arrivals hall (ground floor of the arrivals building). Our driver will assist you to the vehicle

Meeting Point Heathrow Terminal 4

The Meeting point in Heathrow Airport Terminal 4 is the “information Desk” which is located at the left hand side of the arrivals floor, near the taxi desk

Meeting Point Heathrow Terminal 5

Terminal 5 meeting point is Information Desk. The information Desk is in the Arrivals area of Terminal 5, on the ground floor. Passengers arriving at the terminal should turn right when reaching the Arrivals area to find the information zone, at the north end of the building. (Near UK Arrivals)
