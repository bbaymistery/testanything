Journey Time between Heathrow and Gatwick:

Heathrow to Gatwick is approximately 45 miles so you should allow up to an hour to arrive to your Destination, Our Drivers use the M25 and M23 for transfers between the airports, Please take into account the Rush hour time which is from about 07:00 till 09:30, so Please allow yourself more time to get to the Airport so there will be enough time to catch your flight.
